## Week 1: Foundation & Fundamentals

### Day 1-2: Course 1 - Gen AI: Beyond the Chatbot
**Course Link:** [Gen AI: Beyond the Chatbot](https://www.skills.google/paths/1951/course_templates/1268)  
**Daily Goal:** Understand gen AI beyond chat interfaces (4 hours total)
- **Hour 1:** Module overview + Introduction to generative AI applications
- **Hour 2:** Real-world use cases, business impact, and chat vs. other gen AI applications
- **Notes:** Focus on identifying where gen AI can add value beyond conversational AI
- **Checkpoint:** Can you explain 3 non-chat use cases for gen AI?

---

### Day 3-4: Course 2 - Gen AI: Unlock Foundational Concepts
**Course Link:** [Gen AI: Unlock Foundational Concepts](https://www.skills.google/paths/1951/course_templates/1265)  
**Daily Goal:** Master core gen AI principles (4 hours total)
- **Hour 1:** How transformers work, embeddings, and neural networks basics
- **Hour 2:** Prompting techniques, fine-tuning, RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
- **Notes:** This is the technical foundation - take notes on key concepts
- **Checkpoint:** Can you explain how LLMs generate text and what prompt engineering is?

---

### Day 5-6: Course 3 - Gen AI: Navigate the Landscape
**Course Link:** [Gen AI: Navigate the Landscape](https://www.skills.google/paths/1951/course_templates/1261)  
**Daily Goal:** Understand the gen AI market and Google's offerings (4 hours total)
- **Hour 1:** Current gen AI landscape, competitors, models comparison (GPT vs. Gemini, etc.)
- **Hour 2:** Google Cloud's gen AI solutions, Vertex AI, Gemini models, enterprise features
- **Notes:** Map out different providers and their strengths
- **Checkpoint:** What are Google's main gen AI products and when would you use each?

## Week 3: Review & Exam Prep

### Day 11: Deep Dive Review - Fundamentals

## 🎯 4 Exam Domains - Study Focus

Map each course to the exam domains:

### Domain 1: Fundamentals of Generative AI
- **Key Courses:** Day 1-4 (Course 1 & 2)
- **Focus:** Core concepts, how it works, terminology

### Domain 2: Google Cloud's Gen AI Offerings
- **Key Courses:** Day 5-6 (Course 3)
- **Focus:** Products, services, architecture, when to use what

### Domain 3: Techniques to Improve Gen AI Model Output
- **Key Courses:** Day 3-4 (Course 2), Day 7-8 (Course 4)
- **Focus:** Prompting, fine-tuning, RAG, optimization

### Domain 4: Business Strategies for Gen AI Solutions
- **Key Courses:** Day 1-2, Day 7-10 (Courses 1, 4, 5)
- **Focus:** Use cases, ROI, implementation, change management

## 📋 Daily Study Tips

1. **Before each session:**
   - Review yesterday's checkpoint question
   - Set a specific time and minimize distractions
   
2. **During study:**
   - Take notes by hand (better retention)
   - Pause videos to process concepts
   - Write your own explanations

3. **After each session:**
   - Answer the checkpoint question
   - Identify 1-2 unclear concepts to clarify next time
   - Connect learning to real business scenarios you know

4. **Weekly:**
   - Review all notes from the week
   - Create a concept map showing relationships
